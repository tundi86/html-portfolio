The Best Movies According to me

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Ultimate Gift

The story of this movie is really touching. It's about a rich guy, who lost everything to realize what does really matter in the life.

Gifted

The main character of this movie is a little girl who is genius in mathematics. It is funny but on the other hand it is also sad and sometimes makes you cry.

A walk to remember

A love story about two teenagers who lives in totally different worlds and the result is a changed life.
